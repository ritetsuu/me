I am lying next to the Fountain of Youth when Quan approaches me, his sword glinting in the sunlight. I feel the power in me ebbing, a constant reminder that I was fading. The cracks in the peace between the humans and the dwarves.

“Hey. I heard about the human kings stirring up war…” Quan trails off, when he sees another strand of my hair turning white.

I nod silently, staring up at the sun that’s way too bright.
